At Sapphire Capital Group (SCG), we specialize in connecting equity investors with the right commercial real estate investment opportunities. Our team values efficiency, precision,  and strong relationships to deliver successful outcomes. We’re looking for a proactive and organized individual to support our investor database and communications.

Responsibilities:
- Contact equity investors by phone and email to stay informed about their investment criteria
- Update investor contact details including names, emails, phone numbers and company website
- Schedule calls for team members with equity investors
- Follow up with investors after SCG shares new opportunities to track interest and gather feedback
- Monitor and document changes to equity investors programs and contacts in the SCG zoho database
- Maintain and update SCG’s zoho equity investor database with accuracy
